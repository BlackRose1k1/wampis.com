FISH-MAN ISLAND SAGA

The Fish-Man Island Arc is the twenty-fifth story arc of the manga and anime One Piece, both the second and last story arc of the Fish-Man Island Saga, and the second story arc of the second half of the series. The Straw Hat Pirates finally arrive at Fish-Man Island. There, the crew learn the history of Fisher Tiger and the ongoing discrimination between humans and Fish-Men, while the New Fish-Man Pirates plot to overtake Ryugu Kingdom and make Fish-Men be recognized as the supreme race.
